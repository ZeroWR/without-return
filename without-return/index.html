Without Return

With the release of Without Returns first full length album Calm Winter, Jack Beltz has set out to re-invent what Without Return is all about. Deciding to strip down these new songs to the bare essentials, and create a new sound with familiar elements, he has re-invented himself and his music. These acoustic, bass and keyboard based songs sound familiar and are based in the tradition of Folk, Rock and Soul music while remaining fresh and new to the listener. When asked about what was next for Without Return Jack replied;

“My goal is to create something that satisfies my fans and me as well. I want to make something that I have never heard before and to keep things moving forward.”

At the end of his song Calm Winter Jack Beltz sings, “I haven’t found the answer...” so hopefully with a little time and a few more songs those answers will one day be found.
